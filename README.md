## ❯ The short version

I notice things that are broken. I find that interesting. I take them apart, figure out how they work, and build something better. That loop is most of what's on this profile.

Right now that means **AI-native software** — agentic workflows, RAG pipelines, voice-first memory tools, and the small connective tissue between raw data and something a person actually uses.

---

## ❯ Principles I refuse to compromise on

> **Own it.** Outcomes, not tasks. If something's broken, I'm fixing it — even if it isn't "my" code.

> **Do the dirty work.** The unglamorous middle is where most of building lives. Cleaning data. Debugging at 2am. Writing the docs nobody reads.

> **Pursue excellence.** Speed first. Never sloppy. Both, every time.

> **Stay curious.** I'm here because the problems are interesting. The day that stops, I stop.
